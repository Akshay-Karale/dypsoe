About Us

About

Dr.D.Y.Patil School of Engineering is one of the most prestigious academic groups in the state of Maharashtra which comes under the aegis of the Dr.D.Y.Patil Technical Campus. Dr.D.Y.Patil School of Engineering. Lohegoan(DYPSOE),Pune,was established in 2010 under DYPTC by the Chairman of D.Y.Patil Group honorable Dr.Ajeenkye D.Y.Patil.The institute offers four UG Programs (B.E), three PG Programs(M.E)and two Diploma programs.
