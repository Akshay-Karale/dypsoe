ADMISSIONS

    • Admission to the Hostel

    As soon as your admission to any course conducted at D Y Patil School Of Engineering is confirmed you can apply for an admission to the Hostel. Do remember that even though we have accommodation for around 500 students, existing students might already be occupying some of these places. Other available places get filled very quickly. Hence It Is advisable to make an early decision

    The application process Is simple. You can obtain the Hostel Admission Form from the D Y Patil Hostel Office, fill It up In all respects and submit the same with the Hostel Accounts authority along with the requisite payment. Once the payment Is accepted your admission to the Hostel will be confirmed.
