Why Us

    • Welcome to D.Y.Patil School Of Engineering Hostel

    The D.Y.Patil School Of Engineering Hostel is not only a place to live and study, but rather a place to interact with fellow students from different programs, form friendships and bonds which will be with you for your lifetime. Our Hostel is a place to interact on variety of topics, spark off novel and original ideas and share them with a wider community of like-minded brilliant and resourceful individuals. The Hostel environment encourages the residents to express themselves and build a sense of identity and community. Since the Hostel residents will be from variety of disciplines, it provides unique opportunities to foster collaboration and interaction amongst learners of these diverse disciplines.

    The Hostel provides unparalleled ease of access to School Of Engineering facilities and resources. The stylishly decorated rooms are a departure from standard hostel rooms. Our Hostel has a capacity around 500+(Extended Soon) students within its various residential schemes from a totally private room to traditional student housing - so there's a wide variety to suit all tastes and budgets. Fully automated, State-of-the-art kitchen ensures clean, hygienic, balanced and nutritious food for students. The Hostel consists of a common dining hall, a common recreation room with a T.V., and common bathrooms. All bathrooms have hot-water facility. Come to the DY Patil School Of Engineering Hostel and discover your own distinct personality. Be a part of the genesis of a new culture, tradition and legacy that the DY Patil School Of Engineering is Spearheading.
