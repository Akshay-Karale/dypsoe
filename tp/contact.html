Training Model of T & P cell

 

• Mock group discussion and Interview by internal senior faculty and industrial experts.

• Maintaining Track of complete campus selection process in terms of rounds of selection process, questions asked in interview, nature of test. Sharing the same with junior batch so that they can prepare in right direction.

• Question bank comprising probable interview questions

• Taking feedback from the panelist from the companies coming for the placement and polishing the students to overcome the shortcomings.

• Motivating the students to learn foreign language

• Arranging pre placements talks, arranging guest lectures, inviting industry experts as a judges/speakers for different college events.

• Guidance by placed students to unplaced and junior students
