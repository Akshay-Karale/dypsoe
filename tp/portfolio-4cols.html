Entrepreneurs Meet-2017

Name of the program: Entrepreneurs Meet-2017
Duration: 1 day
Date: 26th July 2017
Time: 10 am to 4 pm.


The Entrepreneurship development club, DYP Technical campus,Lohgaon has arranged a one day
Entrepreneurs Meet on 26th July 2017.

The session started at 10:00 am by welcoming the guest of honors Mr. SubhashTalekar (well known as
Mumbai Dabbawala), Mr. FarrhadAcidwala (Founder of Rockstah Media) and Mr. Nitin Godse (CEO of
Excel gas & Equipment private Ltd.).

The event was held in two sessions.1st sessionincluded welcoming of the guests, introductory speeches by Dr. S.S. Sonawane (Director, DY Patil Technical Campus), Dr. E.B. Khedkar (Vice Chancellor, Ajeenkya DY Patil University), Mr. Anil Jahir (Public Relation Officer) & the dignitaries Mr. Sushant Patil (Advisor, DY Patil Technical Campus), Mr. NileshVishwanath (MD, Ayurveda), Dr. Ashok Kasnale (Principal, Dr. DY Patil SOE&T) and Mrs. Shweta Sharma (Training and Placement Officer).

The session mainly included speech by Mr. SubhashTalekar, where he told the success story of the Mumbai Dabbawalas from the beginning till getting worldwide recognition.

Then the audience was addressed by the next speaker, who being a TedX Speaker and also known as the youngest entrepreneur, Mr. FarrhadAcidwala. During this he shared his past experiences on how he perused his interest in the right direction and how he was able to manage the time and always tried to be multitasking in life. Lastly he took up a Q&A session in which he cleared out the queries of students.

The post lunch session included talk by Mr. Nitin Godse about his journey from a small vegetable seller to a 40 crore business.
The session was concluded by Dr. NileshVishwanath, MD (Ayurveda) & founder of Nirwanic path of life and vote of thanks was given by Mrs. Shweta Sharma (Training & Placement Officer). Ajeenkya DY Patil International School is determined to bring a revolution in the education industry with its vivid curriculum and infrastructure in near future.
