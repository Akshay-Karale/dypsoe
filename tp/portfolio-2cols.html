Prime Time Research Media Pvt. Ltd.
Global Education Excellence Awards, 2017


Date: March 05, 2017


One of the India's leading market research, brand management and consultancy services company, Prime Time Research Media Private Limited organized the "Global  Education Excellence Awards 2017" to felicitate India's leading  Schools, Colleges, , researchers, individuals and organizations that have displayed exemplary excellence as Education  across the globe. The event was held on 5.03.2017 at Hotel Ramada Pawai  Hotel in Mumbai. In this regard, we are pleased to inform that we got winner in the category of "Among Best Private Engineering College for Placement in Maharashtra".
 
Global Education Excellence Awards, 2017 recognized more than 175 Preschools, Higher secondary school , ,Pharmaceutical, Poly technic c ,Engineering and Management colleges in India. for demonstrating innovation, dedication, creativity and excellence in the Education  sector. The awards were the result of tireless efforts of Prime Time Research spread over 3 months, which included nomination, comprehensive market research, opinion survey, web research, categorization of the nominations based on various parameters and finally choosing the winners though a panel of distinguished jury.

Shree Sandeep Patil,Former Cricketer of Indian Cricket Team and Mr Ranjan Negi Former Hockey Player and  goalkeeper of Indian Hockey Team . He would later be involved with the development of the 2007 hit film, Chak De India were the Chief Guests for the event who gave away the award certificates to the title holders for various categories.

Addressing the gathering, Chief Guests for the night echoed the view that "awards for recognizing services are an essential part of every industry be an organization or individual". Receiving an award indicates honor and respect that encourages and instills confidence. This initiative from Prime Time to promote Education  Excellence and recognize the achievers was commended by all the festeemed guests.

Various institutes of all over India were participated of this Event likeKarela,MP,UP,Gujrat,West Bengal,asam,Odissa etc. This is a great start to our college for admission and invites reputed companies in our campus. These awards intend to inspire others towards big achievements.
